This project requires GPIO usage. A basic rotary encoder requires 4 pin connections:

1)  5v power
2)  Ground
3)  CLK
4)  DT

GPIO numbers may vary depending on other peripherals that may already be utilising the GPIO.

1)  5v power    PIN2
2)  Ground      PIN6
3)  CLK         PIN16 (GPIO23)
4)  DT          PIN15 (GPIO22)
